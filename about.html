About Me

Front-end developer based in Zürich, Switzerland

Work

After my basic commercial training, I gained many years of professional experience. Among others, I worked at Pfizer AG in marketing, at Degussa International AG as an assistant, and at the municipality of Thalwil as an administrative employee.

During these years, I was entrusted with the organization of several projects. This sparked my interest in project management. I deepened my knowledge with specific extra-occupational education courses in project management as well as in related fields like marketing, communication, business information technology, and accounting.

However, my strength and passion lies in project management itself. I love to organize and to keep the overview even in turbulent situations, to connect the dots, and to find creative solutions. Since 2015, I have been working at the City of Zurich, where I am in charge of organizing different projects.

As I’ve always been interested in programming, I attended online courses to be able to apply my creativity also to digital projects. Therefore, I now look forward to realizing projects in this area in the future.

Personal Life

I am 38 years old and live with my partner in Zurich.

In my spare time I like to be creative, so painting, decorating, or designing and building furniture are among my hobbies. I also love to dance, especially to Latin-American rhythms, and to swim, preferably in Lake Zurich during summer.

I also like to organize theme parties, especially our legendary Halloween event. But I also enjoy to cook and bake for guests at other occasions.

During vacations I usually explore far away countries, both on land and under water while snorkelling.
