Personal Life

I am 38 years old and live with my partner in Zurich. In my spare time I like to be creative, so painting, decorating, or designing and building furniture are among my hobbies. I also like to organize theme parties, especially our legendary Halloween event. But I also like to cook and bake for guests at other occasions. During vacations I usually explore far away countries, both on land and under water while snorkelling. I also love to dance, especially to Latin-American rhythms, or to swim, preferably in summer in Lake Zurich.
